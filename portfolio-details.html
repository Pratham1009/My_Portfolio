Project Details

Project information

  • Category: Toursim
  • Aim: Boost Tourism in India
  • Project date: 01 March, 2023
  • Project URL: Do Visit
  • Developed an interactive dashboard with a primaryfocus on enhancing user engagement and experience.
  • Established the dashboard with the aim of assisting travelers in exploring prominent spots across India within a budget of 1 lakh.
  • Engineered features to facilitate cost-effective travel planning, empowering tourists to experience diverse destinations without breaking the bank.
  • Integrated functionalities for real-time expense tracking, enabling travelers to stay within budget constraints and plan their trips more efficiently
  • Utilized data analytics to provide valuable insights into popular destinations, empowering travelers to make informed decisions about their travel itineraries.

Plan Breakdown

  • Gold Plan: Ideal for budget-conscious travelers, this plan provides a snapshot of India's beauty, covering essential destinations.
  • Silver Plan: Enhance your journey with this plan, offering additional attractions and a deeper immersion into local culture.
  • Platinum Plan: For a more comprehensive experience, the Platinum Plan combines premium accommodations with curated and exclusive experiences.
  • Luxury Plan: Indulge in the epitome of luxury travel, with exclusive accommodations and personalized services to make your journey extraordinary.
